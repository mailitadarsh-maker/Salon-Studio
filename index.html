Who We Are

LUMÉ Salon & Studio was founded on the philosophy that hair styling and skin grooming are expressions of self-care and artistry. We offer a modern, gender-neutral sanctuary designed with premium comfort in mind.

Our stylists, barbers, and nail estheticians are vetted experts, staying at the forefront of contemporary fashion, techniques, and sustainable beauty formulations.
